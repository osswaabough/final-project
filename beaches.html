these are boats that you should visit



Whitehaven Beach, Australia -Location: Whitsunday Island, Queensland -Highlights: Known for its stunning white silica sand and crystal-clear waters, Whitehaven Beach offers breathtaking views and is perfect for swimming and snorkeling.

Grace Bay, Turks and Caicos -Location: Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Islands -Highlights: Grace Bay boasts powdery white sand and calm, turquoise waters. It's a paradise for beach lovers, with excellent conditions for swimming and water sports.

Anse Source d'Argent, Seychelles -Location: La Digue Island, Seychelles -Highlights: Famous for its unique granite boulders, soft white sand, and shallow, clear waters. This beach is a photographer's dream and ideal for snorkeling.

Navagio Beach, Greece -Location: Zakynthos Island, Greece -Highlights: Also known as Shipwreck Beach, Navagio is known for its dramatic cliffs, turquoise waters, and the remains of a shipwreck. It's accessible only by boat.
